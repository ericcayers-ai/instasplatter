# InstaSplatter

## Product
Local-first dual suite for turning ordinary captures into navigable 3D Gaussian splats, and for AOI-driven flood analysis on the same machine. Zero-config by default; every knob available underneath.

## Audience
Photogrammetry-curious creators, survey/drone operators, and flood-risk practitioners on Windows workstations with a GPU. They open the app to start a job in seconds, not to configure a pipeline.

## Register
product

## Platform
web (Tauri desktop shell)

## Voice
Direct, calm, technical without jargon-stacking. Prefer verbs that match the task: Drop, Reconstruct, Export, Run flood. Never hype.

## Brand lane
Capture bay / pigment on glass. Teal pigment is the brand signal; warm brass marks live progress. Surfaces stay out of the way of the viewport.

## Anti-references
Generic purple SaaS dashboards, cream editorial landing pages, nested card grids, emoji chrome, bounce animations, broadsheet density.

## Primary jobs
1. Drop a video or photo folder → watch a splat form → export.
2. Switch to Geospatial → draw AOI → run flood → export products.
3. Opt into Experimental only when they mean to.
